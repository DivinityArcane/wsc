wsc

wsc is a chat client for dAmn-like chat servers.

The chat client uses WebSockets, HTML, CSS, Javascript, and iconic. By default, it does not use Flash.

Features

wsc.dAmn has the following features:

  • Autojoin: automatically join channels when you connect
  • Auto rejoin: automatically rejoin a channel when you get kicked
  • Timestamps: messages can have 24 hour and 12 hour timestamps
  • Away messages: have the client automatically respond to messages while you're away
